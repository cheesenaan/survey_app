    We are Sensing when we:

  • Taste food
  • Notice a stoplight has changed
  • Memorize a speech
  • Follow steps in a plan

    • We are introverting when we:

  • Come up with a new way of doing things
  • Think about future implications for a current action
  • Perceive underlying meaning in what people say or do
  • See the big picture

    • Do you rely on your five senses and want concrete, practical data to work with? Or do you trust your intuitions without necessarily building upon a solid foundation of facts?








    Basis for ComparisonSensingIntuition
    Focus Sensors focus more on the present (today, this week) or the past than the future. Intuitives focus more on the future than the present or the past.
    TendencySensors tend to be practical and down-to-earthIntuitives tend to be imaginative and innovative
    SpeakingSensors prefer to talk about what is happening or what has happened. They are more concerned with the details and will have more descriptive or literal details in their conversation. Intuitives prefer to talk about what things “mean” or symbolize. They are more concerned with the overall big picture and can gloss over a lot of the sensory details
    SpeakingSensors prefer to speak literallyIntuitives prefer to speak metaphorically
    BoredomSensors will get bored more quickly talking about theoretical or abstract conceptsIntuitives will get bored more quickly talking about day-to-day, practical topics
    doSensors like to “do” things. They aren’t likely to sit and just think or daydream. They’re more likely to be working on a project, playing a game, watching a movie, cleaning, building, always “doing”Intuitives like to analyze. They want to think, reflect, study, experiment, innovate. They can be action-oriented, but that action almost always lines up with their future-oriented vision
    ThinkSensors tend to think, speak, and do things in a linear fashion. A leads to B which leads to C.Intuitives like to start a story or project with the “big picture” in mind and then fill in with details as necessary
